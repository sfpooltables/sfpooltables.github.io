How Ratings Work

Each table rating is the average of the scored categories below. Venue rating is the highest-rated table at that venue. Table ranks are sitewide, and tied table ratings share the same rank.

Scored Categories

Cost

  1. 5 Affordable: $1 per game or less
  2. 4 Average: $2 per game
  3. 3 Expensive: $3+ per game

Cloth Condition

  1. 5 Excellent
  2. 4 Very good
  3. 3 Average
  4. 2 Fair
  5. 1 Poor

Rail Condition

  1. 5 Excellent
  2. 4 Very good
  3. 3 Average
  4. 2 Fair
  5. 1 Poor

Rolloff

  1. 5 Level table
  2. 3 Occasional rolloff
  3. 1 Major rolloff

Ball Condition

  1. 5 Excellent
  2. 4 Very good
  3. 3 Average
  4. 2 Fair
  5. 1 Poor

Cue Ball

  1. 5 Standard
  2. 4 Magnet ball provided
  3. 3 Heavy, magnet ball functional
  4. 1 Heavy, magnet ball not functional

Lighting

  1. 5 Excellent
  2. 4 Very good
  3. 3 Average
  4. 2 Fair
  5. 1 Poor

Obstructions

  1. 5 No obstructions
  2. 4 Minor obstructions
  3. 3 Moderate obstructions
  4. 2 Major obstructions
  5. 1 Heavily obstructed

Not Scored

Make, table size, cloth color, pockets, venue notes, and photos are shown for context but do not affect the table rating.
